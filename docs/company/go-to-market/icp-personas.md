# ICP & Personas (Pack)

This document aggregates the initial ICP and personas for IntelliFlow CRM.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

### Primary ICP

- Small-to-mid teams shipping SaaS products
- Needs lightweight CRM + automation
- Values governance, security, and observability

### Buying Triggers

- CRM data is messy / stale
- Lead follow-up is inconsistent
- Need measurable workflow automation without vendor lock-in

## Personas

### Persona: Founder/GM

- Wants visibility and predictable outcomes
- Cares about revenue pipeline and delivery cadence

### Persona: Sales Lead

- Needs consistent follow-ups and reliable reporting
- Wants less manual admin; more time in conversations

### Persona: Ops/RevOps

- Owns process and reporting
- Needs taxonomy, dashboards, and enforceable rules

### Persona: Engineering Lead

- Needs clean integration points and stable APIs
- Cares about quality gates and incident readiness
