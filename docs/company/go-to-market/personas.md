# Personas

## Founder / GM

- Success: faster revenue cycles; predictable execution
- Pain: unclear pipeline quality; inconsistent delivery

## Sales Lead

- Success: consistent follow-up; clear pipeline stages
- Pain: manual data entry; stale records

## RevOps / Ops

- Success: trustworthy reporting; clean taxonomy
- Pain: fragmented systems; inconsistent definitions

## Engineering Lead

- Success: stable integrations; safe automation
- Pain: “mystery” automation; no evidence trail
