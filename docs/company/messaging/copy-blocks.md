## Feature Blocks

### Feature Block: Governance

**Headline**: Validation gates you can actually rely on.

**Body**: Enforce data quality at entry, not after the fact. IntelliFlow CRM's
validation gates check every record, every workflow action, and every automated
decision — so your dashboards reflect reality, not hope.

**Supporting detail**: Schema checks, duplicate detection, confidence
thresholds, and human-in-the-loop triggers. All auditable. None opaque.

---

### Feature Block: Automation

**Headline**: Automation with an evidence trail.

**Body**: Automate lead scoring, follow-up reminders, and data enrichment using
explicit rules and validation evidence — not opaque “black box” changes. Every
automated action is logged with its reasoning so your team can review, adjust,
or roll back at any time.

---

### Feature Block: Observability

**Headline**: See exactly what your CRM is doing.

**Body**: Full OpenTelemetry instrumentation, Prometheus metrics, and Sentry
error tracking built in from day one. When something goes wrong, you see it
immediately — not in the next QBR.

---

### Feature Block: Modern Stack

**Headline**: Built for teams that care about their tools.

**Body**: Next.js 16, tRPC, Prisma, and Supabase — a modern, open stack with
type safety from database to frontend. Clean architecture. Easy integrations. No
vendor lock-in.
