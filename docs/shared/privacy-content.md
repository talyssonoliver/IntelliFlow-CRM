## Information We Collect

We collect account registration details, workspace configuration data, product usage telemetry, and support communications that are necessary to provide IntelliFlow CRM. We do not collect more information than is required to operate, secure, and improve the service.

## How We Use Information

We use personal data to authenticate users, deliver CRM workflows, monitor reliability, investigate abuse, respond to support requests, and meet legal and contractual obligations. Where we rely on consent, customers can review linked policy information before taking action.

## Sharing and Subprocessors

We share data only with service providers and subprocessors that help us host, secure, monitor, and support IntelliFlow CRM. Every processor relationship is reviewed for security, confidentiality, and data-governance obligations before production use.

## Retention and Security

We retain data according to documented retention policies, customer agreements, and regulatory requirements. Security controls include encryption in transit, encryption at rest, role-based access control, audit logging, and privacy-by-design reviews for new features.

## Your Rights

Depending on the applicable law, individuals can request access, correction, export, objection, restriction, or deletion of eligible personal data. Requests that affect regulated or contract-bound records may be fulfilled within the limits of legal retention obligations.

## Contact Us

If you have privacy questions, data-subject requests, or concerns about this policy, contact the IntelliFlow privacy team at privacy@intelliflow-crm.com. We review policy changes before release and publish an updated version identifier whenever the policy changes materially.
