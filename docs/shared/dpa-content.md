## Introduction and Parties

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the IntelliFlow CRM Service Agreement and governs the processing of personal data by IntelliFlow CRM Ltd ("Processor") on behalf of the customer ("Controller").

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] This DPA is entered into pursuant to Article 28 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council (the "General Data Protection Regulation" or "GDPR").

## Subject Matter and Duration of Processing

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The subject matter of the data processing is the provision of the IntelliFlow CRM platform services as described in the Service Agreement. The duration of processing is coterminous with the Service Agreement, unless otherwise required by applicable law.

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] Upon expiry or termination of the Service Agreement, all personal data shall be returned to the Controller or securely deleted as instructed, within 30 days.

## Nature and Purpose of Processing

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The Processor shall process personal data solely for the purpose of providing the services defined in the Service Agreement, including CRM data management, AI-assisted workflows, and analytics reporting.

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] Processing activities include collection, storage, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, erasure, and destruction of personal data as necessary to deliver the contracted services.

## Types of Personal Data

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The personal data processed under this DPA includes, but is not limited to: contact information (names, email addresses, telephone numbers), business relationship data, communication records, and interaction history managed within the CRM platform.

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] Special categories of data as defined by GDPR Article 9 are not intended to be processed under this agreement. The Controller is responsible for ensuring no such data is submitted to the platform.

## Categories of Data Subjects

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The categories of data subjects whose personal data is processed include: the Controller's customers and prospects, the Controller's employees and contractors who use the platform, and third-party contacts stored within the CRM system.

## Obligations and Rights of the Controller

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The Controller shall ensure that personal data submitted to the platform has a lawful basis for processing under applicable data protection law. The Controller is responsible for responding to data subject rights requests and for notifying IntelliFlow CRM of any instructions that may affect the processing activities.

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The Controller retains all rights to the personal data processed on their behalf and may instruct the Processor to correct, delete, or export data at any time.

## Sub-processors

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The Controller grants general authorization to engage sub-processors. The current list of sub-processors is available at intelliflow-crm.com/legal/sub-processors. The Processor shall notify the Controller of any intended changes to sub-processors at least 30 days prior.

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] All sub-processors are bound by data processing agreements that impose equivalent data protection obligations to those in this DPA.

## Data Subject Rights Assistance

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The Processor shall assist the Controller in fulfilling obligations to respond to requests from data subjects exercising their rights under GDPR, including rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.

## Security Measures

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The Processor implements technical and organisational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including: encryption of personal data at rest and in transit, access controls and authentication mechanisms, regular security assessments and penetration testing, and incident response procedures.

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] IntelliFlow CRM maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance and aligns security practices with ISO/IEC 27001. Detailed technical measures are available upon request.

## Data Return and Deletion

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] Upon termination of the Service Agreement or written request from the Controller, the Processor shall delete or return all personal data and delete existing copies, unless retention is required by applicable law.

## Audit and Compliance

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] The Processor shall make available to the Controller all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this DPA and allow for and contribute to audits, including inspections, conducted by the Controller or an auditor mandated by the Controller, subject to reasonable notice and confidentiality obligations.

## International Transfers

[PLACEHOLDER — Legal review required] Any transfer of personal data outside the European Economic Area shall be subject to appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, unless an adequacy decision is in place for the destination country.
